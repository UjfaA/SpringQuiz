Account types:

With Basic account you will be able to take part in the quiz - answer questions and see a score at the end.

With Contributor's account you will be able to take part in the quiz, but also to add and remove questions that you added.

With Moderator's account in addition to the above, you will be able to see and delete questions added by anyone. You will also have access to some stats about the quiz.

With Administrator's account you will have full access. (recommended)
